Intel

Where are the dates?

Expeditions are scheduled once the minimum number of members have joined. You will be notified when your Expedition hits its threshold and goes live.

When do I pay?

When your Expedition goes live member availability will be assessed and a date selected. Only after the date is confirmed, and you accept, will you be charged.

Is transportation provided?

Yes. For all Expeditions, transportation is provided to and from the Facility via luxury coach.

What is your refund / cancellation policy?

Due to the significant upfront costs of the expedition we cannot provide refunds. If you are unable to attend, however, you may transfer your spot on the Expedition to another worthy individual.

How dangerous are Expeditions?

All driving and shooting activities are conducted under the supervision of trained professionals in a controlled environment. We fully expect every member of the Expedition to return in perfect health.

Is there a minimum age or any other requirements to join?

Yes, all Expedition members must be over the age of 21 to participate. For Expeditions that include driving, all members must hold a valid drivers license.

Is The Expedition rain or shine?

Yes, it is. Always. Every time.
