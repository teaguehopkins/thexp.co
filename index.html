Base Camp

Expedition members will meet at an underground venue near the intersection of 16th and K St. in Washington, D.C.

Members are provided with access cards in advance that will grant entry to Base Camp on the day of the Expedition.

Facility

This Expedition will travel to an undisclosed location in central Virginia with over two miles of track, three firearms ranges, a mock urban setting, and a 3,000 foot fully ballistic capable shoot house.

The Expedition will end at the Mandarin Oriental in Washington D.C. where out-of-town members have the option to secure lodging at the special Expedition rate.

Local Expedition members will be provided a car service to return home from the Mandarin at their discretion.

X08

Intel

Do I need an invitation code to join?

Yes, an invitation code is required to join this Expedition. If you do not have a code, contact ops@thexp.co to request one.

How many people
will be on the Expedition?

Space is extremely limited and this Expedition can only accommodate 32 members.

Can I share my invitation code
with a friend?

Yes, but we ask that you do so on a limited personal basis. For example, do not post codes publicly to social media.

Do I get to keep the gear?

Yes, every member receives one custom Arc'teryx jacket, one pair of classic aviator sunglasses by Randolph Engineering, and one pair of full grain calfskin driving gloves.

Do I get to keep the equipment (car and guns)?

Unfortunately, no.

Is lodging included for out of town Expedition members?

Lodging is not included, however, we have secured a special Expedition-only rate at the Mandarin Oriental in Washington, D.C. Contact ops@thexp.co for information on how to book.

Is transportation provided?

Yes. Expedition members are provided with black car service from their home or hotel to Base Camp in the morning, and back from the Extraction point when the experience concludes. Transportation to and from the Facility is also provided via luxury coach.

What is your refund / cancelation policy?

Due to the significant upfront costs of the expedition we cannot provide refunds. If you are unable to attend, however, you may transfer your spot on the Expedition to another worthy individual.

How dangerous is this Expedition?

All driving and shooting activities are conducted under the supervision of trained professionals in a controlled environment. We fully expect every member of the Expedition to return in perfect health.

Can I purchase a spot on The Expedition as a gift?

Yes, this is an option in the checkout process. If you are purchasing the experience as a gift for someone, we will not notify that person until a date you specify.

Is there a minimum age or any other requirements to join?

Yes, all Expedition members must be over the age of 21 to participate and hold a valid drivers license

Is this Expedition rain or shine?

Yes, it is.

Additional Questions?

Please contact ops@thexp.co.
